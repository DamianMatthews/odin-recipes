Baked Potato

Description

This recipe is simple and healthy.

It requires very few ingredients to make a meal.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Butter
  • Bacon bits
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Bake the potato for 50 mins at 375F
  2. Cut open the potato and stuff with cheese
  3. Spring the bacon and butter over the potato
  4. Cool let down for a few minutes and it's ready!
