Chicken Alfredo

Description

This recipe is flavorful.

Note: this recipe contains gluten and traces of nuts.

Ingredients

  • Noodles of your choice
  • 1 TBSP Butter
  • 1c Heavy cream
  • 1/2c Parm cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles to completion
  2. Combine butter, cream, cheese, and cook over medium heat for 10mins
  3. Poor the sauce over the noodles
  4. Add some grilled chicken and it's ready!
